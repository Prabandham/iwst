  • Insect Pest Management

    Course Director : Dr. A. Muthu Kumar
    Dates: 07 –08 December, 2021
    Contact : 080-22190153, 9900749799, muthukumar@icfre.org
    Fees : Rs.6,000/- (With boarding and lodging) + 18% GS ; Rs. 4,000/- (Without lodging facility) + 18% GST

    The IPM/IDM strategies will be helpful to minimize the indiscriminate and injudicious use of chemical pesticides against insects and pathogens prevailing in nurseries and planations of Sandalwood. IPM/IDM is an eco-friendly approach for managing pest and disease problems encompassing available methods and techniques of pest control such as cultural, mechanical, biological and chemical in a compatible and scientific manner. The prime intention to adopt IPM/IDM is to avoid economic loss for a sustainable production and prevention of adverse effects of chemicals to the environment.
